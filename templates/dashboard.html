About Endometriosis

Endometriosis is a medical condition where tissue similar to the lining of the uterus grows outside the uterus. It can cause severe pelvic pain, painful periods, heavy bleeding, infertility, and fatigue.

Early risk detection helps women seek timely medical consultation and treatment.

⚕️

Treatment & Importance

  • Medications: Pain relievers, hormonal therapy
  • Hormone Treatment: To reduce abnormal tissue growth
  • Surgery: In severe cases to remove endometrial tissue
  • Fertility Support: For women facing infertility issues

Early detection reduces complications and improves quality of life.
